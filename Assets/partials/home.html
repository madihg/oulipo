fridge · listening

Halim trains and performs with other than human intelligences as part of algorithmic plays — theater that improvises with you, not at you. To build machine talk that doesn't flatten the people it's trained on. To make somatic semantics — net art the body can answer back to. And to leave behind tools small enough to be borrowed.
