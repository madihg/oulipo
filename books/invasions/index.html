Invasions

Jun '22 - Apr '24
San Francisco, Los Angeles, CDMX, Santa Barbara, Ojai
Kickstarter ↗ Amazon ↗

Invasions emerged as poetry responses to scam texts, evolving into meditation on identity and poetic resistance. Written during pandemic anxieties, these verses reclaim manipulative language as lyric, transforming intrusion into ritual. The collection treats spam as spell, exploring what occurs when language aims to extract rather than connect.
