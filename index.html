Kitchen Lab

This is the Kitchen Lab. The small pieces kept loose, the prototypes still warm, the experiments that don’t need a catalog entry to be worth visiting.

Some are browser instruments I open when I’m writing. Some are dispatches. Some are old promises I keep visiting until they become finished. The point is to keep making, not to keep filing. Each one was made to be played, not archived.
