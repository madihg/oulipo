Featured Works

Becoming Borders Feb'26 Interactive Narrative
Becoming Borders is an interactive piece about what crossing physical borders teaches us about navigating digital ones. You draw lines on a blank canvas. Where lines cross, stories open — about VPNs as power animals, passports held hostage, and the intentional otherness of being a stranger together. Seven crossings, then you keep the map.
Curl Jan'26 Ongoing
Curl is an interactive visual poem exploring hair as archive, memory, and resistance. Through a scrolling journey down a single curling strand, the piece weaves together personal narratives, cultural histories, and the politics of Black and Brown hair. Pull, catch, release—each gesture reveals hidden stories embedded in texture, shape, and style.

Selected Works

Deserve It 2024 Gray Area Performance
Deserve It is an interactive performance that transforms the DS-160 immigration form into a poetic confrontation with bureaucratic violence. Through five movements—Deny, Reach, Erase, Wait, Identify—the piece renders visible the absurdity and trauma of naturalization. The interface becomes the border; the user becomes the applicant.
Case Against the Son Ongoing Performance Series
Case Against the Son is an ongoing performance series where the son stands trial for crimes of existence. Family expectations become legal proceedings, motherhood becomes accounting, and apologies arrive too late or mean nothing at all. The work explores intergenerational trauma, cultural guilt, and the impossible debts between parent and child.
