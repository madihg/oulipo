Keynotes & Workshops

Provocations on AI, language, and creative infrastructure for teams that want to build with care. Keynotes are 30–60 minutes; workshops are hands-on, half-day to five-day formats.

Keynotes

I speak about AI, language, and care. Not lectures — provocations designed to clear the noise and reopen space for creative thinking inside complex teams.

  • Keynotes that Make Room — clearing the noise so the work can speak.
  • Creativity as Infrastructure — patterns, rituals, tools you can keep.
  • Artist as Hacker — curiosity over control, intuition over optimization.
  • Rewiring the System — selected case studies in feedback, joy, trust.
