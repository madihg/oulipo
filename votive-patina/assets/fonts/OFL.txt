Amiri
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Copyright (c) 2010-2024, Khaled Hosny (khaledhosny@eglug.org)
Version used: v30 (sourced via Google Fonts / fonts.gstatic.com)

Amiri is a classical Arabic typeface in Naskh style. It is based on
the Amiria font used by Bulaq Press (Egypt), one of the finest examples
of traditional Arabic book typography.

OFL Reserved Font Names: "Amiri"

EB Garamond
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Copyright (c) 2017, Georg Duffner and Octavio Pardo
Version used: v32 (sourced via Google Fonts / fonts.gstatic.com)

EB Garamond is an open-source revival of Claude Garamont's famous
humanist typeface from the 16th century. This project was initiated
by Georg Duffner in 2010 and continued by Octavio Pardo.
