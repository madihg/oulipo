Carnation.exe

May '25 — European Artist Program, Paris, Barcelona

Carnation.exe is the first round of the "Singulars" tournament. Carnation is a performance-poem where a human poet and a fine-tuned language model duel daily. Each writes on a shared theme: the poet with time, the machine in an instant. Both poems are displayed, and the audience votes with pink dots, digital carnations. Winning poems feed the other side, creating a loop of learning, rivalry, and remembrance where poetry becomes exchange.
