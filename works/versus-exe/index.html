Versus.exe

Nov '25
Mozilla AI Residency, San Francisco
Live site ↗

A structured poetic confrontation between human and machine with daily voting cycles and mutual reinforcement. Just like in Carnation.exe, the poet duels a machine on themes proposed by the audience. The model, trained on an anthology of English poetry, responds almost instantly. Both poems are printed, hung, and kept anonymous. The audience votes. When the human wins, the machine is updated. When the machine wins, the poet adjusts.
