Whomp

Jun '22 — Online

Whomp is a poetic AI simulacrum fine-tuned on Halim Madi's voice and five queer poets. It turns scam texts into viral, uncanny verse. A meditation on authorship, surveillance, and queer futurity, Whomp explores how language multiplies, echoes, and outlasts the body. It's poetry as virus, poet as occupier, an eternal alter ego speaking to audiences not yet born.
